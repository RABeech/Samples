Get ready to go the distance with performance running gear. When it comes to your run, the right gear makes all the difference. You'll need running shoes that are fine-tuned to your unique gait mechanics and apparel that keeps you cool, comfortable and protected from the elements. Running gear that's well-fitted, comfortable and protective helps you stay focused on the course ahead. Prep for the big race with running accessories, including headbands, running socks and water bottles. Whether you're just getting started or you're a seasoned marathoner, we're prepared to help you find running shoes and more gear you need to be your best. Do you often find yourself wondering how to choose the right running gear to get started running? Upstate Running is here to help!

Running Shoes: Your running footwear is the most essential item for your workout. And you need a pair that's fitted to your unique needs as a runner. Select from neutral, stability and motion-control shoes, as well as shoes for distance running, off-road running and barefoot running. The latest running shoes come with a variety of performance-engineered features. There are a number of factors you need to consider before selecting the best running shoe for you. Always determine your gait cycle and foot arch types before selecting your shoe.

Running Apparel: Dress for the season with both warm-weather and cold-weather running apparel. Layer up or down with jackets, long-sleeve tees and leggings. Running clothing is generally crafted from technical fabrics that keep you feeling fresh on the course. And many pieces are versatile for wear during other athletic activities.

Running Accessories: Every runner knows it's the details that matter. Stay hydrated with water bottles or use hair and wristbands to wick away sweat. And outdoor runners can stay safe with the help of reflective running vests and accessories. The latest wearable technology can give your run an edge. Keep pace with activity trackers, pedometers and GPS watches. Many of these running technology devices enable you to track your run, count calories and much more.
